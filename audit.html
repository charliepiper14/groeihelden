Campaign Structure Analysis & Optimization Opportunities
STRUCTURAL PROBLEMS IDENTIFIED
  • ABO Fragmentation: 15+ ad sets in Boost & Convert causing budget dilution and inconsistent optimization
  • Testing Pipeline Bottleneck: Winners sitting in testing campaign too long before moving to main
  • Duplicate Campaign Purpose: Multiple LTO campaigns with overlapping objectives
  • Inconsistent Naming Conventions: Makes performance tracking and reporting difficult
  • No Clear Creative Testing Framework: Ad variations not being systematically tested

WHAT IS WORKING

  • Testing to Scaling Framework: Concept is sound, execution needs improvement
  • Advantage+ Targeting: Allowing Meta to optimize audience
  • Video-First Approach: Top performers are all video content
  • NL/BE Focus: Geographic targeting is appropriate
  • Traffic Retargeting: Efficient E0.12/LPV building audiences

NEEDS IMPROVEMENT

  • ABO Structure: Consolidate from 15 to 5-7 ad sets
  • Budget Distribution: Winners not receiving enough spend
  • Creative Refresh Rate: Fatigue setting in on NL/BE audience
  • Testing Velocity: Move winners to main within 7 days
  • Monitoring Frequency: Need daily checks, including weekends

CRITICAL FIXES

  • Automated Rules: Implement CPR-based auto-pause rules
  • Naming Convention: Standardize across all campaigns
  • Conversion Tracking: Verify Meta vs Backend alignment
  • Budget Caps: Implement daily/weekly spend limits
  • Creative Pipeline: Establish 2-week ahead production
